Jakob Falin Shows You How To:

Catch A Pokemon

Step 1: Choose Your ball

Step 2: Weaken The Pokemon by using a move. For example Pikachu knows

  • Thunderbolt
  • Quick Attack
  • Iron Tail
  • Electro Ball

Step 3: Capture The Pokemon

To capture the Pokemon throw your pokeball at it if it is weakened enough the ball will click and little stars will come out to signify that you caught it.

Step 4: CELEBRATE!
